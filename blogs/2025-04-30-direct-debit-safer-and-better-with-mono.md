Mono Direct Debit provides an automated, secure way for businesses to collect recurring payments directly from customer bank accounts. The platform layers user consent verification, BVN authentication, small deposit authorization, bank-grade encryption, transaction limits, and real-time fraud detection to ensure businesses get paid on schedule.
