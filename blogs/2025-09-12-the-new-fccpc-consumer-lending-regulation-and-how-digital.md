The Federal Competition and Consumer Protection Commission introduced new digital lending regulations in Nigeria, effective October 22, 2025, with fines of 50-100 million naira or 1% of annual turnover for non-compliance. The article outlines requirements including identity verification, creditworthiness assessment using financial data, and secure loan recovery via direct debit mandates.
